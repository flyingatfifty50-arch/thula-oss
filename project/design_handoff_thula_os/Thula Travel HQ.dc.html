South Africa · Cape Town, Addo & Franschhoek

Cape winters (late June – early July) are mild by day, cold by night, and on the wet side. The safari side at Addo runs drier with chilly dawns. Pack warm layers, a waterproof, and something cosy for early game drives.

Cape Town
City, ocean and Table Mountain — the peninsula drive, the Waterfront, and whale season starting in June.
Addo
Malaria-free Big Five country — game drives from the doorstep, dinner under the stars.
Franschhoek
The wine tram, estate lunches, and French Huguenot village charm in the Cape winelands.

Client dashboard — master template

This is a client's home base while they're away: their day-by-day plan, all bookings, where to eat, local know-how, and a space to capture memories. Every new trip starts from this shape — see the mobile app and client website built from it already.

How to clone this for each client
  1. Duplicate the master template.
  2. Rename it for the client + trip (e.g. "The Naidoos, Vietnam, March 2026").
  3. Fill in the emergency box, trip-at-a-glance, and populate the four sections: itinerary, bookings, restaurants, packing.
  4. Share it so the client gets a clean, login-free mini-site on any device.
  5. Delete these instructions from the client's copy.
